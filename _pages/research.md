My research lies mainly in harmonic analysis, potential theory, special functions, and PDE-related analysis.

## Main Research Directions

### Harmonic and Hyperbolic Harmonic Functions

I study sharp inequalities, boundary behavior, integral means, and Hardy-type properties for harmonic and hyperbolic harmonic functions in the unit ball.

### α-Harmonic Functions

A central part of my current research concerns α-harmonic functions associated with the Liu–Peng operator in the unit ball. My work focuses on Schwarz-type estimates, Riesz–Fejér inequalities, invariant mean-value properties, and boundary behavior.

### Fractional Calculus and Special Functions

I have worked on conformable fractional calculus and its applications to special functions, including Bessel, Gauss hypergeometric, and Legendre-type functions.

### PDE-related Analysis

I am interested in analytical methods connected with elliptic partial differential equations, potential theory, and function spaces in higher-dimensional domains.
