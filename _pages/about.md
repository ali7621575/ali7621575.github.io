I am **Ali M. Youssef Ahmed El-Helaly**, a Teaching Assistant and academic researcher in Mathematics.

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in Mathematics at **King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals (KFUPM), Saudi Arabia**. I am also a Teaching Assistant in the Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Science, **Sohag University, Egypt**.

My research interests include **harmonic analysis, α-harmonic functions, hyperbolic harmonic functions, fractional calculus, special functions, complex analysis, and PDE-related analysis**.

My current research focuses on function theory and potential-theoretic problems in the unit ball, especially sharp inequalities, boundary behavior, and related properties of harmonic, hyperbolic harmonic, and α-harmonic functions.

## Research Interests

* Harmonic Analysis
* α-Harmonic Functions
* Hyperbolic Harmonic Functions
* Potential Theory
* Fractional Calculus
* Fractional Differential Equations
* Special Functions
* Complex Analysis
* PDE-related Analysis
