## Brief Profile

Ali M. Youssef Ahmed El-Helaly is a Teaching Assistant and academic researcher in Mathematics. He is currently a Ph.D. student in Mathematics at King Fahd University of Petroleum and Minerals, Saudi Arabia.

His research interests include harmonic analysis, α-harmonic and hyperbolic harmonic functions, fractional calculus, special functions, complex analysis, and PDE-related analysis.
